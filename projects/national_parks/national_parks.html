In 2017, the Great Smoky Mountains was the most visited national park, followed by the Grand Canyon, Zion, Rocky Mountain and Yosemite respectively. Together visits to these five parks accounted for 36% of the total 85,451,798 park visits that year. Across the United States and its territories, there are 417 national parks.

* National Park recreational visits data was published by the National Park Service in Annual Visitation Highlights, accessed August 15, 2018.
